Ohio State's annual tech innovation and entrepreneurship showcase.
Friday, April 8, 2022 6:00 PM - 8:30 PM

Hosted in person this year at the Longaberger Alumni House.

This showcase highlights outstanding and innovative technologies developed by Ohio State faculty, students, and alumni. ShowOHI/O brings together invested parties from across Buckeye Nation for an evening that celebrates and supports tech entrepreneurs making an impact.

Event Information

Join us friday, April 8, 2022 6:00 PM - 8:30 PM this year as we return to hosting ShowOHI/O in person at the Longaberger Alumni House! Enjoy food and beverages while checking out students, staff, faculty, and alumni who will be sharing their developing or improving technologies! The dress code will be business formal. All food and beverages will be included.
