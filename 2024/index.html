Event Information

🚀 Join us for an exclusive evening dedicated to showcasing groundbreaking technologies cultivated by the brilliant minds of Ohio State's faculty, students, and alumni! ShowOHI/O is more than a networking event—it's a celebration of innovation, entrepreneurship, and the vibrant tech coming from Buckeye Nation.

📅 Date: Fri, Apr 12, 2024
🕒 Time: 6pm
📍 Location: Nationwide & Ohio Farm Bureau 4-H Center (Near Schottenstein Center on Ohio State campus).

Eligibility Criteria

Anyone is welcome to apply to present at ShowOHI/O. In the past, ShowOHI/O has hosted a wide variety of endeavors - ranging from hackathon prototypes to established startups. Research innovations, small businesses, and tech-focused student groups are also welcome to present. The only strict requirement is that the presenting party must have some affiliation to The Ohio State University, or its surrounding communities. Additionally, if you are affiliated with Techstars Columbus you are more than welcome to apply! If you have any questions about eligibility, please email us at hackathon@osu.edu.

About

OHI/O is a student organization that is committed to fostering a tech culture at Ohio State and surrouding communities in central Ohio.

ShowOHI/O is OHI/O's tech entrepreneurship showcase, where Buckeye Nation's latest innovations are recognized and shared.
