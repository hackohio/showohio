Presenters

Share your tech idea, project, or startup in a supportive environment. Presenters will receive a table at the event to share and demonstrate their products. Click here to complete the presenter application. The deadline to complete the application is Fri, Mar 29.

Eligibility Criteria

Anyone is welcome to apply to present at ShowOHI/O. In the past, ShowOHI/O has hosted a wide variety of endeavors - ranging from hackathon prototypes to established startups. Research innovations, small businesses, and tech-focused student groups are also welcome to present. The only strict requirement is that the presenting party must have some affiliation to The Ohio State University, or it's surrounding communities. If you have any questions about eligibility, please email us at hackathon@osu.edu.

Resources

ShowOHI/O invites a variety of organizations from the community to host resource tables that may be of interest to entrepreneurs and event attendess. If you are interested in hosting a resource table, please email us at hackathon@osu.edu.

Awards

We present the Buckeye Choice Award every year to the best ShowOHI/O project, as selected by event attendees. Winners will receive a cash prize to further there project.

Logistics

Logistics, including parking information and a schedule, will be posted closer to event day. Please email us at hackathon@osu.edu if you have any questions.

About

OHI/O is a student organization that is committed to fostering a tech culture at Ohio State and surrouding communities in central Ohio.

ShowOHI/O is OHI/O's tech entrepreneurship showcase, where Buckeye Nation's latest innovations are recognized and shared.
